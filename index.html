Inazuma Archon

Shogun 雷電将軍

Raiden Shogun is a character in Genshin Impact who hails from the Inazuma region, one of the in-game regions. She is known as the Shogun and is a powerful and enigmatic figure who rules over Inazuma. Her character design is inspired by traditional Japanese and samurai aesthetics.

The Electro Archon known as "Baal". Baal is the ruler of the Inazuma region, which is the Electro region in the game Genshin Impact. Baal is a strong and enigmatic character in the Genshin Impact storyline.

About Her

Personality

Raiden Shogun is portrayed as a stern and noble leader. She carries herself with a sense of duty and responsibility as the ruler of Inazuma, and she is deeply committed to her vision for the nation's future. Despite her stoic demeanor, she is also shown to be caring and compassionate toward her people. Her character is complex, torn between her aspirations and the consequences of her actions

Role

Raiden Shogun is a playable character in Genshin Impact. She wields a polearm and specializes in the Electro element. Her combat abilities revolve around unleashing powerful Electro attacks, and her Elemental Burst ability, Secret Art: Musou Shinsetsu, is a notable part of her combat kit
